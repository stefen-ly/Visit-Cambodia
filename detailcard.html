Phnom Penh

Highlights

  • Wat Phra Kaew
  • Wat Ratchanadda
  • Wat Pho
  • Golden Mountain
  • Chatuchak Weekend Market

An increasing number of tourists visiting Bangkok each year has proven that Bangkok is always a popular city for tourists.

For major tourist attractions in Bangkok, most of them are historical or religious sites such as temples. There is beautiful architecture, wall painting, and historical significance, such as Wat Phra Sri Rattanasamaram (Phra Kaew), Wat Pho Chetuphon Wimon Mangkalaram, Wat Arun Ratchawararam, Wat Sra Ket, and Golden Mountain.

Other notable sites include Wat Ratchanadda, Wat Traimit, Wat Benchamabophit, Wat Bowonniwet Vihara Suthasan, and Wat Thep Wararam.

There are also many places to relax like palaces, museums, parks, and markets such as Chatuchak Weekend Market, Sampeng Market, Yaowarat, and Phahurat Night Market, as well as upscale shopping areas.
